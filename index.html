About 3C Ventures

A development firm built around thoughtful coastal living.

3C Ventures is a luxury coastal development firm focused on creating architecturally refined residences throughout Brunswick County's premier island communities.

Our approach combines strategic land acquisition, thoughtful design direction, and disciplined project execution to deliver homes that reflect both timeless coastal architecture and modern luxury living.

From Ocean Isle Beach to Holden Beach, each project is designed to embrace its surroundings while delivering elevated craftsmanship, exceptional functionality, and long-term value.

Vic Cianci

Co-Founder

Vic Cianci is a co-founding member of 3C Ventures with more than 14 years of experience spanning real estate, construction, and technology-driven organizations. With a career rooted in development and operational leadership, he brings a disciplined, forward-thinking approach to every project and investment opportunity.

Born and raised in the Cleveland area, residing in Aurora, Ohio, Vic comes from a family of entrepreneurs whose influence shaped his passion for business, innovation, and creating long-term value. His experience across both construction and software-based companies has provided a unique perspective that blends execution, efficiency, and strategic growth.

Through 3C Ventures, Vic is committed to delivering thoughtfully designed projects, fostering meaningful partnerships, and creating enduring value within the communities the firm serves.

vic@3cvent.com

Mike Cianci

Co-Founder

Mike Cianci is a co-founding member of 3C Ventures and a licensed electrical contractor with more than 15 years of experience in the construction and construction management industry. His hands-on expertise and technical background provide a strong foundation for overseeing complex projects with a focus on quality, efficiency, and execution.

A Cleveland native now residing in Brimfield, Ohio, Mike has built his career around delivering dependable results while maintaining strong relationships throughout the construction process. His contracting background and deep industry knowledge play a key role in the successful development and management of projects at 3C Ventures.

mike@3cvent.com

Ryan Casano

Co-Founder

Ryan Casano is a co-founding member of 3C Ventures with a background that blends legal experience, real estate, and construction management. He brings more than six years of experience in the real estate and construction industry, contributing a strategic and detail-oriented perspective to the firm's operations and growth.

Originally from Austin, Texas, and now residing in Akron, Ohio, Ryan attended law school at The University of Toledo and practiced law in Texas before transitioning into the real estate and construction sector. His diverse professional background helps support 3C Ventures' commitment to thoughtful development, strong partnerships, and long-term value creation.
